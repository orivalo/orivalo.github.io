Conclusions & Analysis

The survey results clearly demonstrate that e-commerce is deeply integrated into daily life, with 90% of respondents having completed an online purchase. The demographic data indicates a particularly strong inclination toward online shopping among younger adults (18-25 age range) and individuals with a college degree or higher.

These local findings accurately reflect broader industry trends. According to reports from the Pew Internet and American Life Project, a vast majority of internet users have purchased goods online. Data from eMarketer and the E-Commerce Times consistently highlights the dominance of large-scale retail platforms like Amazon and fast-fashion websites like Shein. Finally, market insights from ClickZ suggest that mobile accessibility continues to drive higher shopping frequencies, matching the weekly and monthly shoppers in our survey.
